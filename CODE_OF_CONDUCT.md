# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be useful, practical, and respectful. Participants are expected to communicate with clarity, patience, and good faith.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of different skill levels, languages, and tool preferences.
- Keep discussions focused on reproducible engineering behavior.
- Provide evidence when making compatibility or quality claims.
- Give feedback on the work, not on the person.
- Respect open-source boundaries and do not post private or unredacted material.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private customer, project, credential, path, or commercial material.
- Spam, unrelated promotion, or repeated off-topic comments.
- Deliberately weakening verification or disclosure-boundary checks.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may restrict participation when behavior harms the project or community.

## Reporting

For sensitive reports, use the security policy in `SECURITY.md`. For ordinary moderation issues, open a GitHub issue and mark it as community/moderation related.
